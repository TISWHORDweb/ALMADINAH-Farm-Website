    About Us

    Al-Madinah Integrated Farms Nigeria Limited

    Al-Madinah Integrated Farms Nigeria Limited was incorporated on 1st July, 2021 as a Limited Liability Company with CAC registration number 1813299. It was established as an agricultural processing and agro-logistics services company with the desire to contribute its quota to the nation's Gross Domestic Product through its operations and services. Secured with highly modernized equipment and facilities, the company is resolute in its drive to reposition the agricultural sector to make food available, accessible and affordable in Nigeria and beyond its shores. AL-MADINAH FARMS is Located in the ancient commercial city of Kano State, North West Nigeria.

    Aims and Objectives

    The company's motivational obligation is to provide food for every man, woman and child; in accordance with sustainable, reliable and cost effective production methods and techniques.
